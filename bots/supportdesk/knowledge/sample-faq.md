# Company Knowledge Base

## Business Hours
We are open Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST. We are closed on weekends and major US holidays.

## Products & Services
We offer three main plans:
- **Starter** ($49/mo): 1 bot, up to 500 automations/month, email support
- **Growth** ($149/mo): 5 bots, up to 5,000 automations/month, priority support
- **Enterprise** (custom pricing): Unlimited bots, dedicated account manager, SLA guarantee

## Refund Policy
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all plans. No questions asked. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.

## Setup & Onboarding
After signing up, you will receive a welcome email with setup instructions. Our onboarding team will schedule a 30-minute kickoff call within 24 hours. Most clients are up and running within 3–7 business days.

## Cancellation
You can cancel your subscription at any time from the account dashboard. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. No cancellation fees.

## Technical Support
For technical issues, email support@botvaultpro.com or use the live chat in the dashboard. Response time: under 4 hours for Growth and Enterprise plans, under 24 hours for Starter.

## Integrations
We integrate with: Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Zapier, QuickBooks, Xero, Shopify, WooCommerce, Gmail, Outlook, and more. See our integrations page for the full list.

## Security & Compliance
We are SOC 2 Type II certified. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We are GDPR and CCPA compliant. We never sell customer data.

## How to Upgrade or Downgrade
Log into your dashboard → Settings → Billing → Change Plan. Changes take effect immediately for upgrades and at the next billing cycle for downgrades.

## API Access
API access is available on Growth and Enterprise plans. Documentation is at docs.botvaultpro.com. Rate limits: 100 requests/minute for Growth, 1,000 requests/minute for Enterprise.
